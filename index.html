Tohru Oikawa

The way of the ordinary

Haikyuu is a Japanese sports manga and animated series featuring Volleyball. It is undoubtly one of the greatest stories ever written with dynamic and fluid characters and their developments and appreciates Volleyball greatly.

In the anime, Tohru Oikawa(back no.1) is the captain of Aoba Johsai Volleyball Team and well respected by his teammates.

Tohru's role in his team is a setter which is a position where he toss to the spikers the ball for them to score points.

He is skilled, competent, observant of his teammates's abilities and can push them to 100% of their potential on the court.

At first glance, Tohru Oikawa seems brimming with talent due to his incredible plays and leadership skills. However, there is a deepr plot here.

There was a time in middle school where Tohru was jealous of his junior.

Tohru Oikawa was a very skilled player even in middle school. However, this new comer, his underclassman, Tobio Kageyama was someone one would consider a prodigy, a genius.

Since Tohru obtained his skillfulness in Volleyball through hard work and dedication, witnessing what talent can achieve first hand devastated him.

Even though he had his moments of weakness he refuses to drown in his misery and worked even harder than before. He achieved best setter of the year in his third year middle school and also went on to win multiple matches till his third year in high school.

Even Though Tohru Oikawa was just a supporting character of the story, I relate to him personally more than the protagonists. Because, growing up, I also didn't have any sort of special talents. I had to work hard for my achievements. So the portrayl of Tohru Oikawa really hit home. And it gives me courage and strength that no matter what difficult tasks life throws my way and it will, I will overcome them with what I have in the best way I can.

"Talent is something you make bloom.
Instinct is something you polish."

-- Tohru Oikawa
